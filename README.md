It tells you not just what is expensive, but how it behaves:AX (High Value, Stable): The VIPs. 💎 Keep tight control. Use automated, frequent replenishment.AZ (High Value, Chaotic): The Risks. 🚩 Carry higher safety stock or use "Make-to-Order" to avoid wasting capital.CX (Low Value, Stable): The Basics. 📦 Buy in bulk. Focus on reducing transaction costs rather than stock precision.2. Forecasting & Maintenance 🔮How to Forecast: * X-Items (Stable): Use Moving Averages. Simple and effective for steady demand. 📏Y-Items (Variable): Use Seasonal Models (SARIMA). Great for capturing trends and holiday spikes. 📈Z-Items (Uncertain): Use Probabilistic Forecasting. Since history is unreliable, focus on "Demand Sensing" (real-time data). 📡How to Maintain:Dynamic Reorder Points (ROP): 🔄$$ROP = (Lead Time \times Daily Usage) + Safety Stock$$Cycle Counting: ⏱️ Don't count everything at once! Count 'A' items weekly, 'B' monthly, and 'C' quarterly.Vendor Synergy: 🚛 Use the "Top 5 Vendors" chart to negotiate shorter lead times for AX items—this directly reduces your required safety stock!
